Building Security
Since 1999

At LINK PLUS, we understand that security isn't just about systems — it's about peace of mind. Founded in 1999 as Lifeguard Integrated Security Solutions, we've spent over two decades building electric door lock systems trusted across India.

From classic remote kits to cutting-edge Wi-Fi smart locks, every product is engineered for Indian conditions — built to last, easy to install, and backed by our legendary after-sales support.
